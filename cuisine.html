Must-Try Dishes

  1. Arepas - Cornmeal cakes that are a staple food in Colombia.
  2. Bandeja Paisa - A hearty dish with beans, rice, meat, and plantains.
  3. Empanadas - Fried pastries filled with meat or cheese.
  4. Ajiaco - A traditional chicken and potato soup.
